Tune how DealScore is calculated for AutoTrader search cards.

Example Tuning Scenarios

  • High-mileage market: increase Miles Per Year to 15000–18000 so normal local trucks are not over-penalized.
  • Low-usage market: lower Miles Per Year to 8000–10000 for stricter expectations.
  • Mileage too dominant: increase Miles Scale to 30000 to soften mileage impact.
  • Mileage not impactful enough: lower Miles Scale to 12000 for stronger mileage influence.
  • Trust KBB more: increase KBB Weight to 15–20 and reduce Miles Weight.
  • Prioritize mechanical wear: increase Miles Weight to 14–18 and reduce KBB Weight.

Preset (KBB-first): kbbWeight 18, milesWeight 6

Preset (Miles-first): kbbWeight 8, milesWeight 16

Preset (Balanced): kbbWeight 12, milesWeight 10
