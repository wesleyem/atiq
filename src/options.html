DealScore Options

Tune how DealScore is calculated for AutoTrader search cards.

Weight behavior: KBB and Miles weights are normalized to a ratio before scoring. Relative balance matters more than absolute size (for example, 20:5 behaves like 4:1).

Hidden Card Toggles

Example Tuning Scenarios

  • High-mileage market: increase Miles Per Year to 15000–18000 so normal local trucks are not over-penalized.
  • Low-usage market: lower Miles Per Year to 8000–10000 for stricter expectations.
  • Mileage too dominant: increase Miles Scale to 30000 to soften mileage impact.
  • Mileage not impactful enough: lower Miles Scale to 12000 for stronger mileage influence.
  • Trust KBB more: increase KBB Weight and reduce Miles Weight (for example, 20:5).
  • Prioritize mechanical wear: increase Miles Weight and reduce KBB Weight (for example, 5:20).
  • Color boundaries: raise Good Deal Threshold for stricter green badges, or lower Poor Deal Threshold for stricter red badges.
